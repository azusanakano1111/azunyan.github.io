About the Lucksack Library

The Lucksack Library is a website created by Wattpad user @pitysack69 to promote his stories. So far, he has published four stories on his account, most of them being based on the Assassin's Creed series of video games.

About the Author

@pitysack69 is an aspiring writer who began writing on Wattpad to hone his skills. He is an avid fan of all things related to anime, manga, light novels and video games. He is also a bit of a history nerd and tries to make his historical fanfictions as historically accurate as possible. He is a massive fan of the show Breaking Bad.
