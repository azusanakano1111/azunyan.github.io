Synopsis

(Y/N) Frye, brother of the Assassin Lydia Frye, has been sent overseas in order to infiltrate the Kingdom of Sauville, a small European country bordered by Switzerland to the north, France to the west and Italy to the east that reportedly holds a Piece of Eden. Undercover as a British student at the St. Marguerite Academy, he encounters Victorique de Blois, a mysterious girl who is also a genius. Together, the two of them solve various mysteries all across Sauville; mysteries that help lead the young Assassin closer to the Piece of Eden, as well as the mysterious Grand Master of the Sauville Rite.

Characters

(Y/N) Frye

(Y/N) is a 15-year old member of the British Brotherhood of Assassins. He is the brother of the late Assassin and World War I hero Lydia Frye as well as the grandson of the Master Assassin Jacob Frye. He is sent to the St. Marguerite Academy in order to infiltrate the fictional country of Sauville and retrieve the supposed Piece of Eden hidden somewhere in the country before the Templar Order does. Image Source.

Victorique de Blois

Victorique is a mysterious student at St. Marguerite Academy that (Y/N) meets at the top of the library tower. She has golden hair and green eyes. She is a very intelligent young lady known for being able to solve even the hardest crimes by putting the "Fragments of Chaos" together using her "Wellspring of Wisdom." Her character is symbolic to two famous Sauville urban legends: "The Golden Fairy of the Library" and "The Grey Wolf who Speaks in the Human Tongue." GIF Source.
