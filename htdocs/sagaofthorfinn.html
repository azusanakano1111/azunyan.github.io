The Saga of Thorfinn the Agile

Currently playing: MUKANJYO by Survive Said the Prophet


Synopsis

The following collection of sagas follows the story of Thorfinn the Agile, an Icelandic Viking and member of the Hidden Ones.

Characters

Thorfinn Arnesson

Thorfinn the Agile is an Icelandic Viking. He is the son of Arne Scarface, a Viking who fought alongside the sons of Ragnar Lothbrok in the legendary Great Heathen Army. Known for his agility that earned him his nickname, Thorfinn is a stealth expert and a trained shot. He is a member of the Raven Clan. GIF Source.

Eivor Varinsdottir

Eivor is a Viking shieldmaiden of the Raven Clan. She was born to Varin and Rosa of the Raven Clan who were both massacred during an ambush by Kjotve the Cruel of the Wolf Clan. She managed to escape the ambush with a wolf's bite on her neck, earning her the nickname "Wolf-kissed." She was adopted by King Styrbjorn of Rygjafylke and became the adopted sister of Sigurd, Styrbjorn's son and new jarl of the Raven Clan after they moved to England. GIF Source.
